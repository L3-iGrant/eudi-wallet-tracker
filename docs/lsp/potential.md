# POTENTIAL

Pilots for European Digital Identity Wallet. Largest of the first wave of Large Scale Pilots, concluded in 2025. Focused on six sectors: government services, banking, telecommunications, mobile driving licences, electronic signatures and health.

## Scope

- Government identification, including SCA for online services
- Banking, account opening, KYC
- Telecommunications, SIM registration
- Mobile driving licence (mDL) under ISO/IEC 18013-5
- Electronic signatures aligned with eIDAS 2 trust services
- Health, including the European Health Insurance Card and patient records

## Participants

Most EU Member States plus selected non-EU partners. See the [European Commission's POTENTIAL deliverables page](https://ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks/sites/spaces/EUDIGITALIDENTITYWALLET/pages/924976339/LSP-POTENTIAL).

## Status

Concluded. Outputs feeding into Architectural Reference Framework versions 2.x and into national wallet implementations.
