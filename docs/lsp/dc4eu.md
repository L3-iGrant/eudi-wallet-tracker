# DC4EU (Digital Credentials for Europe)

Education and social security focused. Concluded in 2025.

## Scope

- Education credentials, including diplomas and micro-credentials
- Social security coordination
- Cross-border trust framework deployment

## Status

Concluded. Outputs land in the European Education Area policy stack and the EHIC successor designs.
