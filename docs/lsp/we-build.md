# WE BUILD Consortium

Phase 2 Large Scale Pilot. Active. Focused on B2B, B2G and B2C use cases including payments and business credentials.

## Scope

- B2B, B2G and B2C process automation
- Payments use cases (succeeds NOBID)
- Business register cross-border attestations
- Qualified Trust Service Provider integrations at scale

## Participants

Nearly 200 organisations: 99 beneficiaries, 74 associated partners, across 27 countries. Includes 20 wallet providers, 13 business register providers, 20 relying parties, 30 QTSPs, 25 PID providers and 25 public agencies.

## Status

Active. Runs until 2027.
