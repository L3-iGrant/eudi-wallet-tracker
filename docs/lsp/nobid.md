# NOBID Consortium

Nordic and Baltic plus Italy and Germany. Focused on EUDI Wallet for payments.

## Scope

- Wallet-based authorisation of payments
- Cross-border interoperability across Nordic and Baltic markets
- Integration with national banking and payments infrastructure (including BankID schemes)

## Participants

Denmark, Germany, Iceland, Italy, Latvia, Norway. Final consortium meeting held in Rome on 26 June 2025.

## Status

Concluded. Findings feed into the WE BUILD payments-focused successor.
