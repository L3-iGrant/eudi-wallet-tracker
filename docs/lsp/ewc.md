# EWC (EU Digital Wallet Consortium)

Focused on Digital Travel Credentials. Concluded in 2025.

## Scope

- Digital Travel Credentials (DTCs) under ICAO 9303-13
- Cross-border check-in, border control and ticketing scenarios
- Integration with existing airline and travel-industry systems

## Status

Concluded. Outputs informed the broader EUDI ecosystem and feed into Phase 2 successor pilots (APTITUDE).
