# Italy

<StatusBadge status="Production (EU Notification Pending)" />

Italy's IT-Wallet system pairs the public IO app with accredited private wallets, with citizens activating via CIE or SPID. SPID has 41.5 million registered users, making Italy one of the most operationally advanced EUDI transition cases in Europe.
